<h2>Ruby</h2>

<h3>Overview:</h3>

<ul>
<li>A dynamic, object-oriented programming language with a focus on simplicity and productivity</li>
<li>Designed for programmer happiness with an emphasis on human-readable code</li>
<li>Known for its elegant syntax and expressiveness</li>
<li>Strong community support and a vast ecosystem of libraries (known as gems)</li>
<li>Popularized by the Ruby on Rails web application framework</li>
</ul>

<h3>Common Uses:</h3>

<ul>
<li>Web development (especially with Ruby on Rails)</li>
<li>Scripting</li>
<li>Prototyping</li>
<li>Automation tasks</li>
</ul>
